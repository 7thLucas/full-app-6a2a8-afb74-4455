Build a client review and approval platform for a marketing agency.

The platform should help agencies share project deliverables with clients, collect feedback, manage revisions, and track approvals in one place. Agencies can create projects, upload files, invite clients, and request reviews. Clients can review deliverables, leave comments, request revisions, and approve final versions.

Each project should have its own review workspace where all files, discussions, approvals, and revision history are organized. The system should support images, videos, documents, presentations, and other marketing assets.

Key capabilities:

* Agency and Client user roles
* Email/password and Google login
* Project creation and management
* Client invitations
* Secure file sharing
* Review and approval workflows
* Revision requests and review rounds
* Comments and discussions
* Visual annotations on files
* Video feedback using timestamps
* Version history and revision tracking
* Approval and rejection decisions
* Activity timeline and notifications

The goal is to replace scattered feedback across email, chat, and meetings with a centralized client review experience that helps agencies deliver projects faster and with fewer misunderstandings.

The application should have a clean, modern, professional interface suitable for creative agencies and their clients.
