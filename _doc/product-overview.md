# Client Review & Approval Platform

## Product Identity
**Type:** B2B SaaS workflow platform  
**Category:** Creative project delivery & client collaboration  
**Primary audience:** Marketing agencies (agency staff) and their clients  
**Design tone:** Clean, modern, professional — editorial, creative-agency caliber  

## Users & Roles

### Agency Staff
Create and manage client projects, upload deliverables, invite clients, request reviews, manage revision rounds, track approval status across all active projects, and maintain a full activity history.

### Clients
Receive project invitations, review shared deliverables, leave annotated comments and visual feedback, request revisions or approve final versions, and track the current status of their projects.

## Core Problem
Client feedback arrives scattered — buried in email threads, Slack messages, Zoom recordings, and chat apps. Files get misplaced. Revision requests go untracked. Projects miss deadlines and budgets because nobody can find the latest version or confirm what was approved, by whom, or against which file. Agencies and clients both lose hours reconciling who said what and which round they're on.

## Core Solution
A per-project review workspace that centralizes every deliverable, annotation, comment, revision round, and approval decision in one place. Accessible to both agency and client, always current, fully traceable.

## Key Capabilities
- **Dual user roles:** Agency Staff with full project management permissions; Clients with structured review-and-feedback access
- **Authentication:** Email/password and Google OAuth login
- **Project management:** Create and manage projects; each project has a dedicated workspace organizing all related files, discussions, and decisions
- **Client invitations:** Invite clients to specific projects via email
- **Secure file sharing:** Upload and share images, videos, documents, presentations, and other marketing assets
- **Review & approval workflows:** Structured request → review → approve or request-revision cycle with formal status tracking
- **Revision rounds:** Multiple review rounds per deliverable; each round is logged with its own version and decision record
- **Comments & discussions:** Threaded comments attached to files and projects
- **Visual annotations:** Draw, highlight, and annotate directly on image and document files for precise, unambiguous feedback
- **Video feedback with timestamps:** Leave time-stamped comments on video files referencing exact moments
- **Version history & revision tracking:** Every uploaded version of a file is retained, labeled, and accessible
- **Approval & rejection decisions:** Formal approve/reject actions create a permanent audit trail
- **Activity timeline:** Chronological log of all project activity visible to both agency and client
- **Notifications:** Real-time alerts for new comments, review requests, approvals, and rejections

## Workflow
1. Agency creates a project workspace
2. Agency uploads deliverables (images, videos, documents, presentations, etc.)
3. Agency invites client and requests review
4. Client reviews files — leaves annotated comments on images, pinned notes on documents, or timestamped video feedback
5. Client formally approves the deliverable or requests revision with specific feedback
6. Agency addresses revisions, uploads new versions, initiates the next review round
7. All rounds, decisions, comments, and file versions are retained permanently in the workspace

## Strategic Positioning
Replaces fragmented feedback across email, chat, and meetings with a single centralized review experience. Helps agencies close project rounds faster, with documented approvals and fewer misunderstandings. Competes in the creative review space alongside tools like Frame.io, Filestage, and Pastel — differentiated by a fully integrated workflow covering all asset types, structured revision rounds, and a professional client-facing portal.

## Design Principles
- Clean, modern, professional interface appropriate for creative agencies and enterprise clients
- Project-centric workspace model — every conversation, file, and decision scoped to its project
- Trust-signaling through transparent version history and formal, documented approval decisions
- Two distinct user experiences: a feature-rich agency dashboard and a streamlined client review portal
